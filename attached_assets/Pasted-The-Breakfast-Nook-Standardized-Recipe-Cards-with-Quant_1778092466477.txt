1. Sunrise Breakfast
Yield

1 Plate

Ingredients
Eggs — 2 each
Bacon — 3 strips
OR
Sausage links — 2 each
Breakfast potatoes — 6 oz
White bread — 2 slices
Butter — 0.5 oz
Prep
Cook eggs to guest preference.
Grill bacon or sausage.
Crisp potatoes on flat top.
Toast bread and butter lightly.
Plate and serve.
