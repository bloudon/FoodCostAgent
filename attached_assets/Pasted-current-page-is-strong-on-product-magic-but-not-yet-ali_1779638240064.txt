Biggest Messaging Problem

Your current homepage leads with labor savings and data entry elimination, not food cost opportunity discovery.

Current lines like:

“Typical weekly data entry eliminated”
“Monthly labor savings for most operators”
“Stop Paying Someone to Type. Start Using Your Phone.”
“Most operators recover the cost of Starter in the first week.”

are useful, but they pull the buyer’s attention toward admin efficiency.

That may attract the wrong mental frame:

“This saves my manager some time.”

The stronger frame is:

“This helps me find where food cost is leaking.”

Labor savings is a supporting benefit. Food cost opportunity is the headline.
