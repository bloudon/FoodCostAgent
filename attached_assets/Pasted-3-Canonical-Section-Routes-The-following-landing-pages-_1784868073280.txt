8. Permissions and Feature Gates

For every canonical route:

Confirm company ownership and tenant isolation remain server-enforced.
Confirm route-level permissions match card and navigation visibility.
Confirm direct URL access cannot bypass role restrictions.
Confirm feature-disabled sections show the approved unavailable state or redirect appropriately.
Confirm global-admin routes remain inaccessible to company users.

Navigation hiding alone is not authorization.

9. Redirect Behavior

Legacy routes should:

Redirect to the canonical equivalent.
Use replace-style navigation where appropriate so the Back button does not loop.
Preserve relevant query parameters where they are still meaningful.
Avoid redirecting to a less-specific landing page when an equivalent child screen exists.
Show a controlled not-found page for routes with no valid replacement.

Keep a centralized legacy-route map for future maintenance.
