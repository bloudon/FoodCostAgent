Claims I Would Soften

A few current phrases are strong enough that I’d recommend softening them until you have beta proof.

“The app does the rest.”

Better:

FnB Cost Pro extracts the data, then your team reviews and confirms it.

“Your system is built automatically.”

Better:

Your system starts building automatically, with review and confirmation during Guided Launch.

“Food cost per portion calculated automatically.”

Better:

Food cost per portion begins calculating as ingredients are matched to inventory and vendor pricing.

“Spot over-portioning, waste, and theft instantly.”

Better:

Surface patterns that may point to over-portioning, waste, shrinkage, or process gaps.

“No manual updates, ever.”

Better:

Reduce manual updates as vendor pricing flows into recipe costs.

You do not want the first beta users holding you to absolute claims before the workflow has been hardened.
