Everything You Need to Control Food Costs

Built by restaurant operators, for restaurant operators. Every feature is designed around real kitchen workflows.

Recipe Costing

Build recipes with drag-and-drop ingredients. Get instant cost calculations with nested sub-recipe support, yield adjustments, and portion sizing. Know your true plate cost down to the penny.

Inventory Tracking

Count inventory by storage location with smart unit conversions. Track on-hand quantities in real time with automated estimates between physical counts. Multiple units of measure supported.

Purchase Orders

Create purchase orders from vendor order guides. Receive against POs with case price entry that matches your actual invoices. Automatic unit price calculations and price history tracking.

Variance Reporting

Compare theoretical food cost against actual usage. Identify waste, theft, and over-portioning with detailed variance analysis. Drill down by item, category, or time period.

Vendor Management

Import order guides from major distributors like Sysco, GFS, and US Foods. Manage vendor relationships, track delivery schedules, and compare pricing across suppliers.

Multi-Store Operations

Manage multiple locations from a single dashboard. Transfers between stores, location-specific pricing, store-level inventory, and consolidated reporting across your business.
